Am I Eligible?

  • You must have ALREADY registered with the NC Board of Elections to vote on Election Day (Tuesday Nov 5th).
    • To check your registration status visit the NC Board of Elections Registration Lookup: https://vt.ncsbe.gov/RegLkup/
      • Keep this tab open we will reference it multiple times
    • Same day registration is only allowed for early voting.
  • If registered you must vote at your designated polling location(s)
    • Your polling location will be listed with other information when you check your registration status


Know Before U Go:

  • Regardless of who you vote for it's important to know about which positions will be on your ballot and which candidate(s) most closely align with your political beliefs.
    • A custom sample/practice ballot will be available when you check your registration status
    • Use an online voter guide to collect information on candidates in a single place.
      • For a smoother and fast voting process consider filling out your sample ballot ahead of time so when you are in the voting booth all you have to do is copy over your choices
      • The States Voter Guide: https://ncvoter.branch.vote/ballot
  • Understand the Referenda on the ballot
    • Referenda can be some of the most challenging and confusing parts of voting so make sure you thoroughly understand what a vote for or against these mean prior to entering the voting booth.
      • Information about these can also be found in The States Voter Guide: https://ncvoter.branch.vote/ballot

 

Time to Vote!

  • Make sure you are going to the correct polling place
  • Bring your ID! You will need a physical ID to vote in the NC General election
    • Physical (not digital) student IDs are accepted. For more information visit: https://www.ncsbe.gov/voting/voter-id#required
  • Catch a ride on Lyft or Uber for 50% off to/from the polling place on Nov 5th
